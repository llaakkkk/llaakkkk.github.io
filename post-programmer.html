What is being a programmer for me?

I think, there are several advantages:

  • Every day learner - technologies change and get better every day, and every programmer should learn new information every day to be on wave.
  • Part of growing community - developers have big community to get help, discuss actual themes and also create meetups, all these things help to feel atmosphere and support of community.
  • Problem solver - all tasks are some problems, which developer should solve like a Superman/woman saves our planet.
  • Well-paid - it is a good benefit for our work.
