How to get prepared for the first job interview?

Hm, I had my first job interview a few years ago and I was too excited and worried at the same time. This is because I didn’t know what to say and what to do.

But now I have a list of tips which help me to prepare for the interview. And I want to share it with you.

Do the research on the company: Just find the site of the company and learn more about what do they do. Check the sites with employees’ reviews for the company and read what do they think . It is also worth to find out more about your “future” job ( main responsibilities/ related experience/ necessary skills and knowledge).

Find examples of the questions that you might be asked: Nowadays a plenty of internet resources suggest examples of job interview questions. Moreover, you can find questions regarding your specific job. For example, I had 2 interviews with similar questions. It was fun.

⠀

Prepare your own questions: Asking questions gives your interviewer a clear understanding that you are interested in this job proposal. Just follow up! This will help you to get some credits.

⠀

Check the road and calculate your time carefully, taking into account traffic jams and public transport timetable. Being late will not work for your favour. Besides, you’ll get annoyed and nervous.

⠀

Just get a good night’s rest to feel fit before the interview and have some time in hand.

⠀ ⠀

And what you do before interview? Are these tips working for you?
