How do I know about IT trends?

All jobs have their own lists of skills and what is needed to be done to hit the top.

Talking about me, I learned programming on my own, as a majority of developers.

  • Socials networks. I’m following key people in programming on Twitter or Instagram, their blogs and youtube channels. They inspire me.
  • Sites with technical articles - there are trusted resources with dev news. Check medium.com.
  • Courses/online courses. Online courses allow you to learn practical materials in short terms. Besides, you can learn, sitting on the couch in pyjamas and bunny slippers. Check coursarea.com, youtube.com & codecademy.com.
  • Teach somebody. Teaching someone is the best way to check myself. It’s a great opportunity to find out fields where I’m an expert or which should be improved.
  • Contributing. Practice, practice and practice. If you are looking for more practical tasks, check open source projects, like github.com. Just find a project with your skills and try to do some issues.
